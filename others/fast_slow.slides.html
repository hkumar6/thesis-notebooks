Fast-slow consensus system¶

We consider the system:

\begin{align} \dot{\mathbf{x}} &=& -D\mathbf{x} + A\mathbf{S}(\mathbf{x}, u) \\ \dot{u} &=& -\varepsilon \end{align}

where $[\mathbf{S}(\mathbf{x},u)]_i = S(x_i,u)$ and $S(x,u) = 2x - x^2 - x^3 + u$. For simulations, we take $\varepsilon = 10^{-4}$.

Periodic orbits¶

We now make a modification to the previous system to get periodic orbits:

\begin{align} \dot{\mathbf{x}} &=& -D\mathbf{x} + A\mathbf{S}(\mathbf{x}, u) \\ \dot{u} &=& -\frac{\varepsilon}{N} \mathbf{1}_N^T\mathbf{x} \end{align}
